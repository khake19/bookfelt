Privacy Policy for Bookfelt

Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Bookfelt ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Bookfelt mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address and basic profile information.
  • User Content: Book entries, reading reflections, notes, emotions, and any other content you create within the app.
  • Voice Recordings: Audio reflections you choose to record (stored locally and optionally synced to the cloud if you have a premium subscription).

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Camera Data: We access your camera only to scan book barcodes (ISBN). Images are processed in real-time and are not stored or transmitted.
  • Usage Analytics: We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage data (e.g., which features you use, app crashes) to improve the app. This data does not include personally identifiable information.
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Bookfelt service
  • Authenticate your account via Google Sign-in
  • Sync your data across devices (if you have a premium subscription)
  • Process book information lookups via Google Books API
  • Transcribe audio reflections using OpenAI Whisper (premium feature)
  • Generate AI-powered book summaries using OpenAI GPT (premium feature)
  • Process subscription payments via RevenueCat
  • Improve app functionality and user experience through analytics
  • Respond to your support requests

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to our secure cloud infrastructure powered by Supabase (for premium users). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Google Sign-in
  • Row-level security policies in our database
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process your data:

  • Supabase: Cloud database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-in: Authentication service (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Books API: Book information lookup (Privacy Policy)
  • OpenAI: Audio transcription and AI summaries (Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management (Privacy Policy)
  • PostHog: Analytics (Privacy Policy)

6. Camera and Microphone Permissions

  • Camera: Used exclusively for scanning book barcodes (ISBN). Images are processed in real-time and are never stored, saved, or transmitted.
  • Microphone: Used only when you choose to record audio reflections. Recordings are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to the cloud if you have a premium subscription.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time from the app settings. This will permanently delete all your data from our servers.
  • Data Retention: We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion, data is removed from our servers within 30 days.
  • Local Data: Data stored locally on your device can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing app data from your device settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Bookfelt is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using Bookfelt, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
